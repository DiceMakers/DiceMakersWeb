Podcast Summary

The world ended a long time ago.
The atmosphere has thinned to the point that it no longer reflects a blue sky except at sunset.
Most organic life is dead, with the only exception being deep sea creatures and the artificial gardens managed by the only inteligent survivors...
Automatons.
Machines built as tools for humanity have outlived their creators.
Now a new civilization is being built.

Using a card game to build the first government, and a dice-curve rpg system (2d6) to roleplay as survivors.

Join Terran and Josh Tuesdays at 5pm as they design the future of this civilization.
