The Design Podcast

Putting knowledge to practice, Terran and Josh use their combined two decades of designing games to build a new TTRPG, including mechanics, world, lore and story.
In the process, they examine existing TTRPG's and explore the briliance in their designs, and share their opinions on game design in general.

Podcast Summary

The world ended a long time ago.
The atmosphere has thinned to the point that it no longer reflects a blue sky except at sunset.
Most organic life is dead, with the only exception being deep sea creatures and
the artificial gardens managed by the survivors.
Automatons.
Creatures built for the use of humanity have outlived their creators.
Now a new civilization is being built.

Using a card game to build the political structures these automatons develop, and
using a dice-curve rpg system (2d6) to roleplay as survivors.

Join Terran and Josh Tuesdays at 5pm as they design the future of this civilization.
